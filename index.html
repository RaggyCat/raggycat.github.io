About Me

I'm a student at Blacksburg High School with a deep love for animals and a commitment to their wellbeing, regardless of their situation or background. As the primary caretaker of two cats and many fish at home, animal care is a natural part of my everyday life.

I dedicate around 8.5 hours each week at Advantage Ranch, where I take horse riding lessons and assist with barn chores, developing a hands-on understanding of large animal care. Every two weeks, I volunteer in cat socialization at the Montgomery County Animal Shelter, helping animals become more comfortable with people and increasing their chances of adoption.

I am actively seeking a veterinary clinic internship to build real-world clinical skills and take the next step toward a career in veterinary medicine.
